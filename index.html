Robinhood Leaflets

Campus advertising for the year's hottest fin-tech startup

Abstract

Robinhood is a zero-commision stock brokerage startup that focuses on democratizing the stock markets for millenials and anyone who wants to participate in the stock market. I am not affiliated with the company at all, I just wanted to design and print some leaflets for potential propaganda to spread around campuses across the country.


The leaf-logo sits well at the top of the paper and FREE is extremely legible.

Process


As these will be distributed in masses, I wanted to word the copy to instantly get people interested in picking one up and reading it. FREE in all-caps, front-and-center on the leaflet seemed like it would do the job. Robinhood continues to receive design awards for their application, so I wanted to include a screenshot of their app which I borrowed from their previous advertising. In order to track how effective these pamphlets were, I setup a link that shortened my referral code down to bit.do/rhood and let me track statistics on how often people visited the link. I'll be sure to update the project with statistics on how effective the propaganda is.

Unfortunately the ink was a little blue-er than the actual turquois of the brand, but the color is still pretty bold. The leaf sits well at the top of the paper and FREE is extremely legible. The dark iPhone weighs the bottom of the page a little too heavy, so I'll swap it out for another white phone in future printings.
